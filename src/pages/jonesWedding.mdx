# Jones Wedding

---

## Passed

- French onion dip, homemade salt & vinegar chips
- Steak tartare lettuce wraps - shallot, capers, cornichons, egg yolk
- Pimento Cheese Arancini, spicy maple
- local crab, rye toast, dill creme fraiche
- scallop ceviche
- Meat & cheese board - traditional accoutrements
- andouille stuffed brioche
- deviled eggs - various toppings: bacon marmalade, caviar

## Starter

- Mushroom Toast - Pan roasted local mushrooms, grilled sourdough, roasted garlic, whipped goat cheese, fresh herbs & lemon

## Salad

- radicchio & endive salad - celery, shaved radish, crispy Benton's ham, Bayley Hazen Blue, citrus vinaigrette

## Veg

- Roasted local carrots, caraway citrus yogurt, honey vinegar, carrot caramel

## Main

- Sous vide and seared Ribeye, charred broccolini, potato gratin, sauce au poivre

## Dessert

- Salted chocolate tart, bourbon caramel, soft-whipped cream
- Lemon bars
- Chocolate pot de creme, graham cracker crumble, toasted meringue, black sea salt
- Creme brulee, lime marmalade

## Alternates

- Grilled asparagus, sauce gribiche, hard boiled egg, crispy Benton's ham
